Jerk Chicken!

Description

This is probably one of the most famous Jamaican dishes out there. Jerk chicken has transcended borders because of it's bold flavors, signature taste, and it's unique culture. There are many dishes out there now like jerk pizza, jerk lasagna, and even jerk chicken egg rolls! I'll teach you how to make this staple of Jamaiacn quisine

This dish isn't too hard to make. The prep time is about 15 minutes with a 30 minute cook time. Where the time sync comes in is the marinating of the chicken. This could be anywhere from 4 hours to overnight to really let the flavors sink into the meat. This recipe will make 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 6 green onions, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper, seeded and minced
  • 3/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of distilled white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon allspice
  • 1 1/2 pounds portioned chicken leg and thigh

Steps

  1. In a food processor or blender, combine the green onions, onion, scotch bonnet pepper, soy sauce, vinegar, vegetable oil, brown sugar, thyme, cloves, nutmeg, and allspice. Mix for about 15 seconds.
  2. Place the chicken in a medium bowl, and coat with the marinade. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours up until overnight.
  3. Pre-heat grill for high heat.
  4. Lighly oil the grill gate. Cook chicen on the prepared grill 6 to 8 minutes, until juices run clear.
  5. Serve while hot.
